I Am The Changer - Cotton Jones

		I couldn't find any tabs including the intro and most of the chords seemed to be in different keys.
I used https://youtu.be/TBeFOBK3SdM to try and see the chords they were using.
I was unsure how to indicate a half step bend, so that's what (2b2#b2) is supposed to represent.
The intro is also played throughout some of the outro. I know this isn't perfect, especially on the
outro, but I think it's pretty close.
